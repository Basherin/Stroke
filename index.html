Stroke Screening Tool

BE-FAST Emergency Check

  • Balance — sudden loss of balance/coordination
  • Eyes — sudden vision changes
  • Face — facial droop
  • Arm — arm weakness or drift
  • Speech — slurred or strange speech
  • Time — Call emergency services immediately

Preventive Risk Factors

  • Age
  • Blood Pressure
  • Diabetes
  • Smoking
  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Atrial fibrillation
  • Left ventricular hypertrophy

For educational and screening purposes only — not a substitute for medical diagnosis.
